Executive Summary 1




Challenge:


Colaberry Inc. faced inefficiencies in their student enrollment process, characterized by manual lead qualification, impersonal communication, and a fragmented onboarding experience. These issues led to delays, lower engagement, and conversion rates, and an inconsistent onboarding process for prospective students.


Approach:


The project implemented advanced AI technologies, including Generative AI for personalized communication, Document AI for automated document handling, and Robotic Process Automation (RPA) for efficient data entry. This comprehensive solution integrated machine learning models for lead scoring, sentiment analysis for engagement, and structured workflows for seamless onboarding.


Outcome:


The Enrollment Pro solution reduced manual effort by 70%, increased lead conversion rates by 35%, and cut onboarding time by 50%. Personalized communication enhanced engagement by 25%, and 90% of new students reported a smooth and satisfying onboarding experience. The scalable architecture ensured the system could handle increased volumes of prospective students efficiently.
